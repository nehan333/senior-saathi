# SeniorSaathi Team Contributions

This document outlines the responsibilities and contributions of each team member involved in the development of SeniorSaathi.

---

## Project Overview

SeniorSaathi is an AI-powered multilingual assistant designed to help elderly and illiterate citizens access government services, understand documents, manage medication schedules, and interact with civic services through voice-first technology.

# Collaboration Guidelines

* Each member should work on their assigned feature branch.
* Changes must be committed with meaningful commit messages.
* Pull Requests/Merge Requests should be reviewed before merging.
* Team members should coordinate integrations regularly to avoid conflicts.

---

# Mission

Our mission is to improve accessibility, digital inclusion, and quality of life for elderly and illiterate citizens through AI-powered technology.

Together, we aim to build a solution that makes government services, healthcare information, and daily assistance accessible to everyone.
